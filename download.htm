Pine Lake Middle School

The Pine Lake Middle School project consists of a new multistory education facility of 145,000 square feet constructed in two major phases. Phase 1 entailed constructing the new building—including installation of all major utility systems and surrounding site work—on the sports fields adjacent to the fully operational existing facility. In Phase 2, the existing facility was demolished to allow completion of new parking areas and sports fields.
